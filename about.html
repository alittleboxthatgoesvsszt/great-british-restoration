About This Project

A comprehensive, internally consistent blueprint for restoring a lean, effective, and sustainable British state grounded in radical minarchism, realism, and mechanical restraints on power.

About the Great British National Restoration Framework

What This Is

This is not a political party manifesto or a collection of isolated policies. It is a complete architectural framework for fundamentally reforming British governance — reducing the state to its essential night-watchman functions while restoring liberty, responsibility, and national continuity.

Philosophical Foundation

The project is built on radical minarchism tempered by realism. We accept human nature as it is, not as we wish it to be. The state is necessary for defence and justice, but it is also inherently dangerous. Therefore every part of the system is designed with hard mechanical restraints against expansion.

Core Objectives
  • Restore sovereignty, fiscal discipline, and national cohesion
  • Return power and responsibility to individuals, families, and local communities
  • Secure Britain’s demographic and cultural continuity
  • Replace distortive taxation and regulation with principled alternatives
  • Build in permanent structural defences against state bloat
Approach

All policies are designed to be interlocking and mutually reinforcing. PETS funds the minimal state. PRUNE reduces its size. PILLAR and Jubilee prevent regrowth. FLIP and Assize of Worth keep justice accountable. The Golden Hurdle and STORK protect the nation’s future.

This is an open-source project. We invite serious scrutiny, debate, and improvement from those who share the goal of a free and sovereign Britain.
